## Page-Level SEO Strategy

### Homepage (index.html)
**Focus**: Brand awareness + top-level keywords
**Keywords**: bachelor's notes, bachelor notes nepal, question bank, TU syllabus, PU syllabus
**Meta**: Targets general searches

### Browse Page (client.html)
**Focus**: Search-driven traffic
**Keywords**: question papers, semester notes, engineering notes, BCA notes, syllabi
**Query params indexed**: ?contentType=note, ?contentType=question-paper, ?contentType=syllabus

### Individual Notes (scanner.html)
**Focus**: Long-tail keyword targeting
**Dynamic content**: Title, subject, author, faculty all influence SEO
**Best practice**: Add descriptive note titles that include keywords

## Recommended Admin Form Best Practices

When uploading notes, use these patterns:

### Title Examples:
- "Mathematics for Engineering - 1st Semester TU Notes"
- "Physics Question Bank - BCA Program"
- "Chemistry Syllabus 2024 - Purvanchal University"

### Subject Naming:
- Use full subject names: "Mathematics" instead of "Math"
- Include semester context where possible
- Standard subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Programming, Database, Networks

### Description:
- Include 1-2 key terms: "Complete notes with solved examples"
- Mention university/program if relevant
- Keep under 160 characters for search preview
